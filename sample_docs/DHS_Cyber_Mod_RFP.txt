1.1 Purpose
The purpose of this solicitation is cybersecurity modernization support services.

1.2 Background
The Department of Homeland Security requires continuous SOC modernization,
FedRAMP aligned cloud operations, and secure proposal support.

3.1 Technical Approach
The contractor shall provide 24/7 SOC monitoring services, automated triage,
incident response, and monthly metrics reporting.

C.3.1 Requirement
The contractor shall provide continuous monitoring and comply with NIST SP 800-171.

M.1 Evaluation
Technical approach is weighted 40 percent. Past performance is weighted 30 percent.
Price is weighted 30 percent.
