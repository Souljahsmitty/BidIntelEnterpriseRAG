1.1 Purpose
This solicitation requests cybersecurity modernization support for a federal agency operations center.

3.1 Technical Approach
The contractor shall provide continuous 24/7 SOC monitoring, automated incident triage, monthly metrics reporting, and an escalation model from Tier 1 through Tier 3.

3.2 Staffing
The contractor shall provide cleared cybersecurity analysts within 30 days of award.

M.1 Evaluation Factors
Technical approach is weighted 40 percent. Past performance is weighted 30 percent. Price is weighted 30 percent.

M.2 Risk Factors
Transition timeline, staffing availability, and FedRAMP authorization status are evaluated risks.
